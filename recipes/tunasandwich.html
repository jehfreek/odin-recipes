Tuna Sandwich

Tuna Sandwich is a sandwich usually made from canned tuna, adding in a salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 can tuna
  • 2 tbs mayo
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 tspn balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tspn dijon mustard
  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 tspn chopped dill pickle
  • 1/4 cup shredded sharp Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Place bread slices in oven to toast
  3. Mix tuna, mayo, salt, vinegar, mustard, dill pickle into a small bowl
  4. Remove bread from oven and place mixture onto bread
  5. Bake for 7 minutes
