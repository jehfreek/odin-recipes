Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken fried rice is the comfort dish of Chinese food. It involves
the frying of rice in a traditional Chinese wok and adding cutlets of chicken
and other vegetables like peas.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of water
  • 3 cups instant white rice
  • 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 12 slices bacon
  • 1 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce, or to taste

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan
  2. Stir instant white rice into boiling water
  3. Cook rice until softened, about 5 minutes
  4. Heat oil over medium heat in a separate skillet
  5. Cook chicken in hot oil until no longer pink
  6. Fry bacon in a large wok
  7. Remove bacon to a plate lined with paper towels to remove grease
  8. Return wok to medium-high heat and combine all ingredients
